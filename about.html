What is Schedule Builder?

Schedule Builder is a program developed
by Gonzaga students for Gonzaga students.

No more late nights deciding which sections
are right for you! Just choose the classes and
we will autogenerate all possible schedules
for you.

All you have to do is find which
schedule works best for you!

Steps

  1. Select your subject
  2. Add your desired course
  3. Select prefered professors
  4. Select prefered times
  5. Add to schedule
